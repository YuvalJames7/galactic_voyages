How safe is space travel with Galactic Voyages?

Safety is our top priority. We maintain a 100% safety record with advanced spacecraft, expert crew, and comprehensive safety protocols. All passengers receive thorough pre-flight training and medical clearance.

What's included in the ticket price?

Your ticket includes transportation, accommodation, meals, guided tours, safety equipment, pre-flight training, and 24/7 customer support. Additional activities and premium services may be available for an extra fee.

How long is the training program?

Our pre-flight training program is 3 days long and covers zero-gravity orientation, emergency procedures, spacecraft systems, and space suit operation. Training is conducted at our Earth Orbit Station facility.

What are the age requirements?

Passengers must be between 18-70 years old. Minors can travel with parental consent and additional safety requirements. All passengers need medical clearance from a certified physician.

Can I cancel or reschedule my trip?

Yes, we offer flexible booking options. Cancellations made 30+ days in advance receive a full refund. Rescheduling is available subject to availability and may incur additional fees.

What should I bring on the trip?

We provide all necessary equipment including space suits, safety gear, and personal items. You can bring small personal items, but all luggage is subject to weight and safety restrictions.

Is travel insurance included?

Basic travel insurance is included in your ticket price. We also offer comprehensive space travel insurance for additional coverage including medical emergencies and trip interruption.

How do I prepare physically for space travel?

We recommend regular cardiovascular exercise and maintaining good overall health. Our medical team will provide specific preparation guidelines after your booking confirmation and medical clearance.

Still Have Questions?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to help with any additional questions.
