Our Story

2075 - The Beginning

Founded by former NASA astronauts Dr. Sarah Chen and Commander Michael Rodriguez, Galactic Voyages started as a small satellite deployment company with big dreams.

2078 - First Civilian Flight

Successfully launched our first civilian passenger to the Moon, marking the beginning of commercial space tourism for everyday people.

2080 - Mars Expansion

Opened Mars Base Alpha and began regular passenger flights to the Red Planet, making interplanetary travel a reality.

2082 - Outer Planet Tours

Launched our Jupiter and Saturn expedition programs, becoming the first company to offer tours to the outer solar system.
